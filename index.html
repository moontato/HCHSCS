Hinsdale Central HS Coding Club


Welcome to Hinsdale Central's Hour of Code! All throughout this week (Dec. 7 - 11), we are encouraging students to participate in some activities that are meant to teacher you one of life's most valuable skills in this day and age: coding!

To get started, click on any of the links below and complete the activity. When you are finished, check off the activity you participated in and move on to the next. It doesn't matter what order you complete them in so long as you finish all of them. Once you've done all of the activities, click next. Fill out the form and hit submit. After that, you're all done!

Have fun!
